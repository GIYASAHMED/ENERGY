NAAC Green Audit : A Pathway to Sustainability

The NAAC Green Audit is an environmental assessment designed to evaluate an educational institution's commitment to sustainability, resource management, and compliance with environmental policies.

What is a NAAC Green Audit?

The NAAC Green Audit is an environmental assessment designed to evaluate an educational institution's commitment to sustainability, resource management, and compliance with environmental policies. Conducted as part of the accreditation process by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), this audit ensures institutions adopt eco-friendly practices and contribute to environmental conservation.

The rapid urbanization and economic development at the regional and global levels have led to several environmental and ecological problems.
